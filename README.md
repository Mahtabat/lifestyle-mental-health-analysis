# Are Healthier Lifestyle Habits Associated with Better Mental Health and Happiness?

## Project Overview
This project investigates whether daily lifestyle habits are associated with mental health conditions and self-reported happiness.
Using observational survey data, the analysis explores how factors such as sleep duration, work hours, screen time, and social interaction relate to well-being.

## Research Objectives
- Identify lifestyle patterns most strongly associated with mental health and happiness
- Compare happiness levels across mental health conditions
- Explore whether lifestyle imbalance is linked to lower happiness

## Hypotheses
1. Happiness differs across mental health conditions  
2. Sleep and social interaction are positively associated with happiness  
3. Lifestyle imbalance (low sleep, long work hours, high screen time) is associated with lower happiness  

## Tools & Skills
- Excel (data cleaning, pivot tables)
- SQL (aggregation, grouping, comparisons)
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, exploratory data analysis)
- Data visualization (Tableau)
